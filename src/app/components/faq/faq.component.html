Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the purpose of this platform?

This platform aims to provide reliable assistance, even in underserved or unfamiliar areas. Our goal is to be a trustworthy resource for you.

Q2: How to Use It

a. How to search for help:

  • Click the "Request for Help" button.
  • Submit the details of the help you need.
  • Our registered buddy will connect with you to provide trusted assistance.

b. How to Register a Business:

  • Click the "Register Your Business" button.
  • Click on the “Signup” button.
  • Fill in required information and click “Register.”
  • Wait for admin approval to manage your business/service.
Q3: Why this platform?
  • Lack of Trustworthy Support: Verified help is often missing, theft/fraud risks exist.
  • Geographical & Access Barriers: Remote areas often lack infrastructure and services.
  • Socio-Cultural Stigma: Mental health, counselling, and emotional support carry strong stigma.
  • New Residents: Newly placed candidates are unaware of local services, food, gyms, libraries, and rentals.
Q4: How You Can Join This Earning & Support Platform
  • Provide a service matching requester's needs and negotiate your fee.
  • Register your business/service. After admin approval, seekers/customers can connect.
  • If you need help, submit your request using the “Request for Help” button.
